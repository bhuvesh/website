Biography

I am a graduate student in the School of Computer Science at the College of Computing in Georgia Institute of Technology working with Prof. Jacob Abernethy. I am interested in Machine Learning Theory and Optimization and I am part of the CS Theory Group. I graduated from IIT Kanpur, India in 2017 with Bachelors of Technology degree in Computer Science and Engineering with a minor in Linguistic Theory where I was advised by Prof. Purushottam Kar and Dr. Prateek Jain. In the summer of 2016, I visited Prof. Raman Arora’s lab in Johns Hopkins University.
